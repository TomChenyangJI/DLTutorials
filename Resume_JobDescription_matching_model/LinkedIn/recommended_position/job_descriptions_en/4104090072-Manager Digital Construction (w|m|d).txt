Manager Digital Construction (w/m/d)



We are looking for you as Manager Digital Construction (f/m/d) for our Risk & Regulatory division as soon as possible. This is what diversity awaits you - you will support leading international construction and infrastructure projects in all phases of digitalization - from strategy development to technical implementation. In addition to strategy and organization, this also includes the further development of our customers' digital landscape/platform, especially in platform strategy, software selection and implementation topics. Architecture planning - You develop architecture roadmaps and blueprints and create concepts for IT development planning for global infrastructure projects. You control the implementation by the global technology and development teams or external companies. You also carry out IT architecture audits. Product development - You are responsible for PwC's own SaaS construction platform. The platform serves as a central collaboration platform for controlling large and complex construction projects. What this means for you is that you are responsible for the platform end-to-end. What you bring with you: You have completed a degree in (business) computer science or (business) engineering with a focus on technology/computer science or a comparable course of study. You have several years of experience as an enterprise architect, solution architect or in technology consulting with a suitable focus. Ideally, you have practical experience with new technologies (robotics, AI, etc.) in the construction industry as well as in-depth knowledge of enterprise architecture methods, governance structures and frameworks (TOGAF, Zachman, etc.). Your profile is rounded off by experience with common technologies, e.g. in the areas of cloud (Azure, AWS or similar), ERP / XRM / DMS and collaboration. PowerBI, PowerAutomate and PowerApps are also no strangers to you. Very good written and spoken English skills round off your profile. When applying, please note that the job requires an average of at least 3 days of travel per week.
