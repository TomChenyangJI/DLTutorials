Position Overview:
Commission and Business AnalystThe main duty of a Business and Commission Analyst is to collect and professionally analyze and examine data about the organization and its' business. After the detailed analysis of data, they try to use it to increase the efficiency of the company's processes and procedures. They also contribute to and pro-actively participate in the improvement of products and services. As a Business Analyst, you'll work in one of the types of business analysis and spend time on the development of data models and the completion of financial and sales analysis. The Commission and Business Analysts earn $74,610 annually on average in the United States.
