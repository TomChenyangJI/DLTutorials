Position Overview:
As a SAP Business Analyst, you will bridge the gap between business needs and technology solutions within an organization that utilizes SAP software. Your role will involve gathering requirements from stakeholders, analyzing processes, and configuring SAP systems to meet business needs. You will also collaborate with IT teams, provide user training, and ensure effective implementation and support of SAP solutions. Strong analytical skills, SAP expertise, and effective communication are essential in this role as a SAP Business Analyst, contributing to streamlined and efficient business operations.
