Position Overview:
A Business Analyst is a professional who serves as a bridge between business needs and technology solutions. They gather and analyze data to understand an organization's business processes, identify opportunities for improvement, and recommend effective solutions. Business Analysts also work on defining business requirements, creating project plans, and facilitating communication between stakeholders. Their role is critical in ensuring that technology projects align with business goals and deliver value to the organization. Business Analysts need strong analytical, problem-solving, and communication skills to excel in their role.
