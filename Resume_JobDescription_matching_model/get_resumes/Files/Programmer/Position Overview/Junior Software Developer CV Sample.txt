Position Overview:
Junior Software DeveloperJunior software developers are responsible for creating, testing, and maintaining software applications under the supervision of senior developers. They work closely with the development team and project managers to ensure that software projects are completed on time and meet the client's requirements. In addition, they troubleshoot and debug software issues, document software design and programming changes, and stay up to date with the latest software development trends and technologies. Junior software developers typically work in a fast-paced, collaborative environment and are expected to continuously develop their skills to advance to senior positions.
