Position Overview:
As an IT Solution Architect, you will be responsible for designing and implementing technology solutions that align with the organization's strategic objectives. Your role will involve analyzing business requirements, assessing existing systems, and recommending solutions that optimize efficiency, scalability, and security. Collaborating with cross-functional teams, you will develop architectural designs, create technical roadmaps, and provide guidance throughout the software development life cycle. The ideal candidate should possess a deep understanding of technology trends, excellent problem-solving skills, and the ability to communicate complex technical concepts to both technical and non-technical stakeholders.
