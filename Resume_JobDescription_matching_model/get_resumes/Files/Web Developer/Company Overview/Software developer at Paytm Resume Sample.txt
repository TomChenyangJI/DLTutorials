Company Overview:

Paytm is a leading Indian financial technology company offering a diverse range of digital services. Founded in 2010, Paytm initially gained popularity as a mobile wallet and payment platform. Over the years, it has expanded its services to include digital payments, mobile recharges, utility bill payments, online shopping, and financial products like insurance and wealth management. With a user-friendly app and a commitment to financial inclusion, Paytm has become a comprehensive digital ecosystem, empowering millions of users to manage their finances conveniently and securely.
