Position Overview:
A Mechanical Robotics Engineer specializes in designing and developing mechanical systems for robotic applications. Responsibilities include conceptualizing, prototyping, and optimizing robotic structures, ensuring precision and functionality. This role involves integrating mechanical components with sensors and actuators, collaborating with cross-disciplinary teams to achieve robotic system objectives. Proficiency in CAD tools, knowledge of materials, and a deep understanding of robotics dynamics are crucial. As a Mechanical Robotics Engineer, you contribute to advancements in automation, artificial intelligence, and manufacturing, playing a pivotal role in the evolution of robotic technologies across various industries.
