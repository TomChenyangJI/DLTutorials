Position Overview:
An Aircraft Engineer is an aviation professional who designs, maintains, and oversees the repair and modification of aircraft. Their objective is to ensure the safety, functionality, and performance of aircraft. They apply engineering principles to assess and address structural, mechanical, and electronic components, ensuring compliance with aviation regulations. Aircraft Engineers play a crucial role in enhancing aviation safety and efficiency, collaborating with a team of specialists and technicians to keep aircraft in optimal condition, making air travel safe and reliable.
